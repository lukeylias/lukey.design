2022

Secure messaging

  • Product Design
  • Web
  • iOS and Android
  • Lead designer

I was the lead designer on a project to introduce in-app messaging so customers could reach support directly from their banking app — one of my favourite projects to date.

We wanted to bring Greater’s reputation for friendly, in-person service into a digital context and make getting help feel more personal and immediate.

This project was deeply rewarding — we helped make banking assistance more human and accessible. Through testing, we found the feature supported a wide range of customers who faced different challenges and accessibility needs.

"I would change banks for this" – User Testing participant / Non-Greater customer.

For a deeper dive, you can view the full design journey and documentation in this figjam file

Impact

  • Over 90% satistifaction from users in testing
  • Estimated 30% reduction in customer support calls
  • Scalable design across Web, iOS and Android

Key Learnings:

  • People are hesitant about using chatbots inside their banking apps
  • Chats can be a high security risk with people giving over personal details
  • When building the same feature across multiple platforms, especially native applications, try and use what comes out of the box. That way, it's easier to maintain in the long term
  • People would rather talk to a real human than a chatbot
