2022

Secure messaging

  • Product Design
  • Web
  • iOS and Android
  • Lead designer

Delivering a messaging feature to help customers reach out when they need support. I was the lead designer in delivering a chat feature that customers could a message to customer support in their app. My favourite feature I had the chance to work on.

Impact

  • Over 90% satistifaction from users in testing
  • Estimated 30% reduction in customer support calls
  • Scalable design across Web, iOS and Android

Key Learnings:

  • People are hesitant about using chatbots inside their banking apps
  • Chats can be a high security risk with people giving over personal details
  • When building the same feature across multiple platforms, especially native applications, try and use what comes out of the box. That way, it's easier to maintain in the long term
  • People would rather talk to a real human than a chatbot

I loved being a part of this project for a few reasons, We got to build messaging in a banking app with a fantastic team. Banking apps tend to do help and support poorly, particularly from within their apps, so let’s do it better. Greater is known for its in-person customer service. How can we bring that feeling into the app?

This was a rewarding project because, through user testing, we found this feature would aid a wide range of customers through the different challenges and problems they may experience.

For a deeper dive, feel free to check out this figjam file which documents the journey and a high level.
